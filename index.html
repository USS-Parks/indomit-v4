Core Capabilities

Built for Operational Sovereignty

Not a toy. Not a proof of concept. A production-grade AI orchestration platform engineered for businesses that can't afford downtime, data leaks, or cost surprises.

›

Centralized Ontology Engine

A unified knowledge graph that maps your organization's terminology, relationships, and institutional memory into a queryable framework. Every department, same language, same source of truth.

›

Closed-Loop Security

No outbound data transmission. Network-Isolated Mode comes standard on every unit, preventing accidental or unauthorized outbound traffic. For classified and regulated environments, TEMPEST-rated air-gap configurations are available as a custom engagement. Your prompts and your outputs never leave the physical walls of your facility.

›

Multi-Model Orchestration

Run multiple specialized models simultaneously. Route tasks to the right model based on complexity, domain, and resource availability. Swap models without rewriting a single workflow.

›

Administrative Load-Lightening

Document generation, compliance checks, scheduling, reporting, correspondence, data entry. The repetitive work that eats 40% of your staff's week, handled by local AI that knows your processes.

›

Predictable Cost Structure

One-time hardware investment and first-year support subscription. No per-token charges. No usage tiers. No pricing changes that force you to rearchitect your operations at a vendor's whim.

›

Plug-In Integration Layer

REST API and webhook endpoints for connecting to your existing systems. ERP, CRM, document management, ticketing. Allodial adapts to your stack, not the other way around.
